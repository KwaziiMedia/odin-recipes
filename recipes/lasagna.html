How to make lasagna

Description

This is so amazing they made a show about a cat who loves this!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water
  • Seasonings

Instructions

  1. Cook and drain ground beef
  2. Stir spaghetti sauce and simmer
  3. Combine cheese, eggs, and seasonings
  4. Assemble lasagna like a sandwhich in a bake pan
  5. bake for 45 minutes
  6. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes
