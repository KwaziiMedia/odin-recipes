Tuna and Corn "Jail Food"

Description

Tuna and Corn with a little spice so easy to make I consider it jail food

Ingredients

  • Canned Corn
  • Canned Tuna w/ water
  • Mayo
  • Crackers of choice

Instructions

  1. Empty most water from tuna and corn
  2. Pour tuna and corn into bowl and mix
  3. Mix 2-3 spoons of mayo into bowl
  4. Grab crackers of choice and enjoy!
